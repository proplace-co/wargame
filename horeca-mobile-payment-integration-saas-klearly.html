1. 💬 THE GRAND NARRATIVE (The Vector)
✦︎ The Shift: Transition to mobile-integrated processing driven by cashless trends and PSD2.
✦︎ The Velocity: Europe online gateway TAM $20.5B; related orchestration CAGR 15-20%.
2. ⌛ THE CHOKE POINT (The Scarcity)
✦︎ The Constraint: Stage 2 (SaaS Architecture) is the primary bottleneck/control point.
✦︎ The Implication: Entities owning the Horeca-specific workflows command the profit pool.
3. 💀 THE EXTINCTION EVENT (The Losers)
✦︎ The Dying Model: Hardware-dependent card readers and mature commoditized tablets.
✦︎ The Mechanism: SoftPOS enables NFC payments via existing devices, eroding reader sales.
4. 💰 THE NEW UNIT ECONOMICS (The Money)
✦︎ The New Margin: Shifts to SaaS fixed-cost layers (70-90% margins) over variable processing.
✦︎ The Winner: Hybrid subscription ($49 ARPU) plus transaction fee models targeting SMBs.
