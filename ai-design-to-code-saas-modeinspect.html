The future of AI Design-to-Code SaaS

Macro Trends : Design-to-Code AI Platforms Surge
1. 💬 THE GRAND NARRATIVE (The Vector)
The Shift: Generative AI enables no-code platforms converting design prompts to production-ready React/Tailwind UI code.

The Velocity: Global TAM $4.9B (2024) growing at 38.2% CAGR to $24.7B by 2029.
2. ⌛ THE CHOKE POINT (The Scarcity)
The Constraint: Stage 4 Platform Economics emerges as primary control point (8.8 score) via network effects.

The Implication: Ecosystem lock-in through plugins and marketplaces creates bottlenecks for enterprise adoption.
3. 💀 THE EXTINCTION EVENT (The Losers)
The Dying Model: Mature Commoditized players lacking proprietary AI for production-grade code (e.g., Base44).

The Mechanism: 25-30% market share erosion as "vibe coding" leaders achieve $200M ARR rapidly.
4. 💰 THE NEW UNIT ECONOMICS (The Money)
The New Margin: Gross margins expanding to 75-92% for Stages 3 & 4.

The Winner: Tiered SaaS at $200/user/mo with usage-based add-ons and CI/CD integrations.
