The future of Sustainable Aviation

Macro Trends : Hybrid-Electric Regional Aviation Surge
1. 💬 THE GRAND NARRATIVE (The Vector)
The Shift: Regulatory pressures from EU Green Deal targeting 55% emission cuts by 2030 drive shift to certified hybrid-electric aircraft.

The Velocity: Sustainable aviation tech sees substantial growth post-2020s; Europe SAM reaching $430M-$880M.
2. ⌛ THE CHOKE POINT (The Scarcity)
The Constraint: Stage 4 (Manufacturing) acts as the bottleneck due to high capital requirements and facilities scarcity.

The Implication: Entities owning certified assembly lines command immense leverage over the 2030 sustainability mandates.
3. 💀 THE EXTINCTION EVENT (The Losers)
The Dying Model: Legacy OEMs (Airbus/Embraer) suffer margin compression in regional turboprops.

The Mechanism: Specialized startups (ZeroAvia/Aura Aero) erode niche regional shares via pure-play sustainable propulsion.
4. 💰 THE NEW UNIT ECONOMICS (The Money)
The New Margin: Profit pool shifts to Stage 6 Aftermarket (Double-digit) and Stage 4 Manufacturing (expanding with volume).

The Winner: Integrated OEMs capturing Stage 4 bottlenecks with Stage 6 recurring revenue lifecycles.
