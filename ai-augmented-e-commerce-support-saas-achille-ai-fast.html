❓ Market DEFINITION

AI-hybrid customer support automation for mid-sized e-commerce brands handling 1,000+ monthly tickets. This market sits at the intersection of traditional BPO (Business Process Outsourcing) and Customer Service SaaS, transforming a cost center into a software-driven high-margin operation.

💬 Our Market THESIS

LEGACY players in the 100B dollar customer support market, like traditional BPOs and seat-based incumbents, are caught in an innovator dilemma, unable to adopt outcome-based AI models without cannibalizing their existing headcount-driven revenue. This paralysis, triggered by the LLM revolution, creates a clear opening to solve the seasonality pain and build a new market leader.

🧠 Our CONVICTION & WAGER on this Market:

🟢 HIGH: Our conviction is high because this market presents a rare alignment of timing and structure. The AI explosion has opened a temporary window for a decisive founder to build a dominant moat through a proprietary data loop where human corrections train the AI to handle the 'long tail' of complex e-commerce issues. This is a land grab.
