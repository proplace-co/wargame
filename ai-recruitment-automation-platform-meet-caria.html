Macro Trends : AI Recruitment Sourcing Surge
1. 💬 THE GRAND NARRATIVE (The Vector)
The Shift: AI-powered recruitment and digital hiring transformation redefining TA software for scaling firms hiring 50+ roles globally.
The Velocity: TAM $25.69B growing to $51.16B by 2032. Europe SAM $1.85B via 74,000 customers at $25,000 ARPU.
2. ⌛ THE CHOKE POINT (The Scarcity)
The Constraint: Stage 2: Talent Sourcing & Candidate Discovery acts as primary bottleneck, commanding highest strategic score of 7.9.
The Implication: Pricing power via premium SaaS (85-95%) and deep-learning moats enable leverage over upstream and downstream.
3. 💀 THE EXTINCTION EVENT (The Losers)
The Dying Model: Mature Commoditized incumbents (Entelo/Loxo) suffer share erosion due to low differentiation.
The Mechanism: Advanced autonomous AI agents and conversational platforms displace standard DB-dependent features.
4. 💰 THE NEW UNIT ECONOMICS (The Money)
The New Margin: Shifting to Stages 2-3 (85-95% & 80-92% margins) via fixed SaaS costs and $10-25/assessment pricing.
The Winner: End-to-end modular AI recruiting SaaS (like Meet Caria) capturing 10% SOM of $185M in scaling Europe firms.
