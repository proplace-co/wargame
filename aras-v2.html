DÉFINITION du Marché

Le marché correspond à la fourniture d'équipements de sécurité de classe entreprise reliés à des architectures logicielles unifiées nécessitant d'obtenir des habilitations obligatoires de raccordement. Structural Assessments

Market & Timing

The Danish commercial physical security market is in an active consolidation phase, with regional integrators being aggregated by platform groups like Prosero to overcome technical fragmentation and satisfy escalating NIS2 compliance requirements.

The timing window is optimal today — NIS2 is not a future threat but an active regulatory forcing function that is already causing property managers to demand documented physical security SLAs from their installation partners, a demand that unaffiliated small integrators cannot satisfy without the backing of a group with certified infrastructure.

The primary timing catalyst is precisely this regulatory pressure: the obligation to produce auditable, standardised security documentation for multi-site commercial properties creates immediate pull for Aras-certified partners and, by extension, for Prosero as an acquirer of Aras.

The critical timing risk is the cloud-native bypass scenario: if a cloud-native access control vendor begins selling directly to installers without requiring proprietary certification, the regulatory urgency that currently validates Aras's gatekeeper role could be partially redirected toward simpler provisioning models within a three-to-five year horizon.

Acquiring now, before this displacement materialises, is therefore strategically correct.

Competitive Advantage

Aras's primary moat is a Certification Moat — mandatory technical training on proprietary NOX and SIMS architectures creates switching costs at the installer level that are both financial (sunk cost of certification) and operational (complete rebuild of security policy configurations if SIMS is abandoned).

The moat strength is MODERATE: it is highly defensible within the current partner ecosystem but structurally vulnerable to two specific attack vectors identified by the Competitive Advantage analyst. First, a competing national wholesaler could replicate distribution services if it ever gained access to NOX hardware specifications — but this is currently blocked by the exclusive distribution agreement.

Second, an international cloud-native security software entrant could shift the gatekeeper role from a local distributor to a cloud vendor, bypassing the physical certification requirement entirely; this is the more credible long-term threat and the one most likely to close the timing window.

On the build-versus-buy question, the verdict is unambiguous: years of accumulated regional integration relationships, the certified partner extranet, and the SIMS software architecture deeply embedded in multi-site deployments cannot be replicated organically by Prosero within a timeframe relevant to the current NIS2 window.

The most dangerous near-term competitive move is ADI Global replicating distribution coverage without the certification lock — but ADI lacks the proprietary certification barrier that makes Aras's position structurally different from commodity distribution.

Synergies détaillées — D'où vient la création de valeur
Chaque carte ci-dessous représente une synergie identifiée entre l'acquéreur et la cible. Le LLM (module 1327) classe chaque synergie dans une des 4 catégories ci-dessous, et fournit explicitement une valeur Y1, Y2, Y3, Y4, Y5 par synergie. Le code n'applique aucun ramp-up automatique — Y6 et au-delà reprennent simplement la valeur Y5 (steady-state). Le ramp-up effectif (montée progressive Y1 → Y5) est entièrement modélisé en amont par le LLM.

Catégories présentes dans ce dossier :
  • Soft Revenue — Cross-sell & upsell sur la base clients combinée. → impacte revenue_addition
  • Hard Cost — Économies d'échelle effectives (mutualisation, redondances éliminées). → impacte une ligne OPEX : rd_opex, sales_opex, marketing_opex, ga_opex, capex
  • Defensive Moat — Acquisition défensive empêchant un concurrent de récupérer la cible. → impacte revenue_addition
  • Structural — Création de valeur structurelle profonde (intégration verticale, élimination de friction). → impacte revenue_addition ou OPEX selon mécanisme
