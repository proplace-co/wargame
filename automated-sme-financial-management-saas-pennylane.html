> PROJECT NEBULA

Market Overview

The Generative AI infrastructure market is currently undergoing a Phase 2 consolidation. While 2023 was characterized by explosive fragmentation, current trends indicate a shift towards vertical integration. Enterprise customers are moving away from "model shopping" towards unified platforms that offer compute, storage, and inference optimization in a single pane of glass.

Key growth drivers include:

  • Rise of localized LLMs requiring specialized edge compute.
  • Sovereign data requirements pushing for on-prem/hybrid solutions.
  • Massive reduction in inference costs (approx -40% YoY).

Competition Summary

[Competitive Landscape Map]

The competitive landscape is bifurcating. On one side, Integrated Ecosystems (Microsoft/OpenAI, Google) are capturing the generalist market. On the other, Specialized Vertical Players are winning in legal, medical, and finance by offering fine-tuned models on proprietary data.

Our target sits in the MLOps Middleware layer, which is becoming crowded. The primary threat is commoditization as major clouds absorb vector storage and orchestration features directly into their console offerings.

Target Company: Nebula Data

Value Proposition

Nebula Data provides a serverless vector database built for high-throughput GenAI applications. Unlike incumbent search solutions, Nebula separates storage from compute, allowing for infinite scaling during peak RAG usage without over-provisioning.

Product & Tech

  • Proprietary Indexing: 10x faster retrieval than HNSW standard.
  • Hybrid Search: Combines keyword (BM25) with semantic search out-of-the-box.
  • Integrations: Native connectors for LangChain, LlamaIndex, and OpenAI.
