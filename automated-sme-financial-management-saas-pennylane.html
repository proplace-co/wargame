Competition Overview

The "Shared View" Advantage

Most competitors choose a side: either they build for the SME (QuickBooks, Xero side) or for the Accountant (Cegid, Sage). This creates a data silo where files must be exported/imported.

Pennylane's Moat: A single database shared by both parties. Changes made by the SME (uploading a receipt) are instantly reflected in the Accountant's ledger. This reduces "chasing information" time by 40%.
