Company Deep Dive

Value Proposition

Jolimoi offers a personalized beauty and wellness shopping experience by connecting consumers with dedicated Stylists who provide ultra-customized recommendations based on individual diagnoses and needs. It aims to simplify beauty routines, promote conscious consumption of quality products, and empower independent Stylists through a Social Selling platform. They promise to help users avoid clutter, reduce ecological footprints with recyclable/biodegradable packaging choices, and discover new brands.

Ideal Customer Profile (ICP):
Individuals seeking personalized beauty, wellness, and nutrition advice; consumers who want quality, conscious (organic, natural, vegan), and multi-brand products; those looking to simplify their beauty routine and avoid impulse purchases; people who value trust and personal recommendations over mass-market advertising. Also, independent entrepreneurs and freelancers who are passionate about beauty products and want to generate income through social selling.

B2B or B2C:
Both. It is B2C for the end consumers who purchase beauty, wellness, and nutrition products through personalized Stylist recommendations. It is also B2B for the independent Stylists ('Social Sellers') who partner with Jolimoi to build their own businesses, utilizing Jolimoi's platform, tools, training, and multi-brand catalog. The platform acts as an intermediary facilitating sales between product brands and consumers, leveraging the Stylists as the sales channel.
