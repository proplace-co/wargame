Value proposition

Pigment provides an integrated planning platform, powered by agentic AI, designed to improve how businesses plan, make decisions, and drive growth. The platform aims to be easy to use yet powerful enough for enterprise-level planning, offering the fastest time to value.

Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

Large businesses and enterprise companies, targeting Finance, Sales, HR, and Supply Chain teams. Users range from C-level executives to individual contributors needing AI-assisted data analysis.

Product

Pigment offers an integrated planning platform with agentic AI capabilities for businesses to analyze, model, and plan. It provides a unified enterprise platform for various business users, particularly within finance, sales, and HR, enabling them to make decisions and drive growth by consolidating data and automating routine tasks using AI.

Feature Encyclopedia

  • Agentic AI for proactive business planning (Supervisor, Analyst, Planner, Modeler).
  • Natively embedded AI agents trained on user's data and context.
  • Integrated financial, sales, workforce, and supply chain planning tools.
  • High-impact performance management and scenario simulation.
  • Highly scalable data engine capable of running multiple models simultaneously.

Business model

Pigment operates on a SaaS (Software-as-a-Service) model based on Licenses sold through Order Forms. Supplemental Services (integration, configuration, training) are also offered via Statements of Work. The model is enterprise-focused, with licenses sold per Authorized User.

Revenue Streams

Fees for Licenses and Supplemental Services are paid according to Order Forms. Pricing is not public and invoices are typically due within 30 days. Fees are exclusive of taxes, and interest may be charged on overdue sums.

Team

The company vision is to build a unified enterprise platform with the fastest time to value for any business user. It aims to create resilient businesses, enable employees to focus on meaningful work, and democratize planning with AI.
